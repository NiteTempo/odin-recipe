A simple recipe for a salmon sushi roll


Ingridients

  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 2/3 cup short-grain rice
  • 1/2 pound Salmon

Instructions

  1. Hand wrap rice and salmon by laying seaweed paper flat and center salmon and rice. Roll with both hands.
  2. Cut the rolled Sushi roll into 1 1/2 inch slices. Enjoy.
